Going For A Walk

Walking meetings were very productive for Steve Jobs. Being outside inspired William Wordsworth's creativity. Aristotle preached while walking, while Henry David Thoreau did his greatest thinking while strolling around.

Walking may spark conversation, strengthen relationships, spark creativity, promote mindfulness, reduce stress, and boost productivity. Walking also benefits both physical and emotional health.
